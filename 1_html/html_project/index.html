The band members are are:

  • Mette Lindberg - Vocals
  • Lars Iversen - Producer & Bass
  • Mikkel Balster Dorig - Guitar
  • Simon Littauer - MPC & Keys
  • Rasmus Littauer - Drums

They have a few albums out:

  1. Fruit - 2009
  2. Out of Frequency - 2012
  3. Bring Us Together - 2014
